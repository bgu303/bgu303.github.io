What is CSGO?

Counter Strike Global Offensive (CSGO) is the latest game of the most popular FPS game series ever called Counter Strike. It is developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. CSGO was released in August 2012. The objective of the game is to beat the other team. There are two teams, 5 players each, Counter Terrorists (CT) and Terrorists (T). Objective for the T's is to plant a bomb and make it explode, CT's objective is to defuse the bomb. Terrorists win if the bomb explodes, Counter Terrorists win if they defuse the bomb. You can also win a round by eliminating whole enemy team. First team to 16 rounds wins the game. Due to the simplicity of the game paired with unlimited skillcap of how good you can be make this a perfect FPS game.
