Rocket league

Cars and balls, what else can you ask from a game? Rocket League is free to play game where you simply put it: play football with your car. It's alot of fun with friends and it's definitely worth the try since it's free to play. It's not hard to get started with it, but the learning curve of the game can be really tough but also rewarding. So what do you get to lose? It's free to play so get Rocketin'!

CSGO

I don't think world's most popular FPS game needs much of an introduction, but for those who have never heard of CSGO you are missing out. CSGO is the newest version of the popular franchise Counter Strike. Recently, a tournament held on Stockholm broke viewer records for the game. So if you are interested in a high skill based First Person Shooter, CSGO might be the game you have been looking for!

League of Legends

League of Legends has been at the top of the E-Sports world for years. League of Legends (also known as LoL) defined the meaning of MOBA. Ever since LoL was released, it has been at the top of Twitch viewer charts. The game has gone through alot of changes that hasn't always made old players happy, but it seems like the game is still going strong in 2021.

Runescape

Probably everyone has heard of Runescape. Ever since it's release in 2001, it has been massively popular. Runescape, as many other games, has gone through alot of changes that didn't sit well with the older playerbase. That's why in 2013, Jagex, the creator of the game, released "Old School Runescape" (also known as OSRS) which rolled back the years to 2007. After somewhat challenging start to OSRS it kicked off and took off in flying colors. Ever since the release of OSRS, it has taken the limelight of the Runescape franchise.

Path of Exile

Want to have hundreds of builds to choose what to play? Play PoE. Seriously, there's so much stuff in PoE this little text box is not gonna do justice to it. It's quite difficult game to get in to, but once you are in you cannot stop. It's played from overhead perspective and your objective is to clear your way through thousands of monsters in Wraeclast to save place called Oriath. Sounds simple enough? Choose a skill you want to use to kill bunch of stuff and play through the campaign to get into seriously vast End Game!

Ring of Elysium

Ring of Elysium is my favorite battle royale game of all times. Sadly the creators of the game didn't think the same. Ever since it's creation in 2018 and good start to a great story for a good battle royale game, the game has been in great decline of players. That's mainly part of the creators because they just don't care about the game anymore. I'm just super sad to see game with such great potential go to waste. I have played alot of battle royale games and nothing beats RoE and its great map called Dione.
