What is Rocket League?

Rocket League is a football videogame you play with a car. The game is developed and published by Psyonix. It was first released for Windows and Playstation 4 in July 2015. Rocket league is a sequel to Psyonix's SuperSonic Acrobatic Rocket-Powered Battle-Cars, which was released in 2008 on Playstation 3. It is a game where you use your car to hit the ball around the field and score points on goals. Rocket League has single-player and multiplayer modes. Rocket League also has different modes, ice hockey and basketball. Rocket League is also very popular esport. Rocket League has cross-platform play so you can play with your friends on different kinds of devices.

Pros

  • Free to play
  • Extremely fun to play with friends
  • Short queue times to get to games
  • Short Games (5mins)
  • Relatively easy to learn the basics
  • Very good training options
  • Doesn't need a good PC
  • Can be played with both controller or keyboard and mouse

Cons

  • Has alot of toxic players
  • Hard to master
  • Can easily make you fustrated
  • Laggy at times
  • Has annoying quick chat that can be spammed
