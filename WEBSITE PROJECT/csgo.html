What is CSGO?

Counter Strike Global Offensive (CSGO) is the latest game of the most popular FPS game series ever called Counter Strike. It is developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. CSGO was released in August 2012. The objective of the game is to beat the other team. There are two teams, 5 players each, Counter Terrorists (CT) and Terrorists (T). Objective for the T's is to plant a bomb and make it explode, CT's objective is to defuse the bomb. Terrorists win if the bomb explodes, Counter Terrorists win if they defuse the bomb. You can also win a round by eliminating whole enemy team. First team to 16 rounds wins the game.

Pros

  • Extremely fun to play with friends
  • Free to play
  • Short queue times to get to games
  • Short Games (5mins)
  • Relatively easy to learn the basics
  • Very good training options
  • Doesn't need a good PC
  • Can be played with both controller or keyboard and mouse

Cons

  • Has alot of toxic players
  • Hard to master
  • Can easily make you fustrated
