What is Path of Exile

Path of Exile is free-to-play action role-playing videogame developed and published by Grinding Gear Games (GGG). It was released in October 2013. Xbox One version was released in 2017 and Playstation 4 version in 2019. Path of Exile (also known as PoE) takes place in a dark fantasy world called Wraelcast. The objective of the game is to go through Wraelcast and its multiple cities in order to save city named Oriath from ancient gods by defeating them. After the campaign of the game, the "real" fun begins. Then you get to explore the endgame content of PoE, which includes mapping, activity where you defeat monsters from a given area. in PoE there's enough endgame content to throw your whole life away!

Pros

  • Free to play
  • Variety in character and build selection
  • Helpful community
  • Frequent updates
  • New League reset (new game mechanic) every 3 months
  • Content for hundreds of hours
  • Looks really good
  • Good story

Cons

  • Requires good PC
  • Single player oriented (can be played in with friends though)
  • The need to complete whole story mode again when creating a new character
  • Really hard learning curve
  • Important Quality of Life upgrades are behing a paywall
  • Badly optimization of the game (can be laggy)
