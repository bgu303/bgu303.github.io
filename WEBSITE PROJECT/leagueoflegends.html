What is League of Legends

League of Legends, commonly referred as LoL or League, is a multiplayer online battle arena videogame published by Riot Games in 2009. League of Legends is inspired by a custom map from another game called Warcraft |||. League of Legends has always been free-to-play. In the game, two teams of five players battle on an arena. Each player is in charge of one Champion. Your goal is to defeat the enemy base called Nexus by working as a team, killing enemies and securing objectives. League of Legends is the most popular esport of all times. It has had millions of player world wide and there seems no stopping to most popular game.

Pros

  • Free to play
  • Has over 150+ champions to play
  • Can be learned against bots
  • Fun to play with friends
  • All paid content is just cosmetics
  • Multiple game modes
  • Doesn't require a good PC

Cons

  • Same problem as most PvP games, really toxic players
  • Trolls have too much power over the game
  • Game times can feel too long when losing
  • Balance issues on Champions
  • Takes long time to learn
  • Frequent client lag issues
