What is Ring of Elysium

Ring of Elysium (also known as RoE) is a free to play battle royale game developed by Aurora Studio and published by TCH Scarlet Limited, both owned by Tencent Games. It was released in September 2018. The objective of the game is similar to all of the battle royale (BR) games, you are supposed to be the last person alive. You start the game with only a pistol and you need to explore the map in order to gain weapons and medics. You win the game by escaping by a chopper by the end of the game. It's the unique mechanics that makes RoE in my opinion stand out from the rest of the BR genre. For example, in snowy map called Dione you can choose a gadget to use during the game, glider, snowboard or grappling hook. Sadly RoE is just a shadow from the glory days of 2018 because of the neglect towards the game by the developers.
