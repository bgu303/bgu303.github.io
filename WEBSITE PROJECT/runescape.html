What is Runescape?

Old School Runescape (OSRS) is massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by Jagex. OSRS servers were launched on 22 February 2013. Mobile version of the game was released in 30 October 2018. Objective of the game is to train your personal character's skills. There are 23 different skills in OSRS. Some of these are combat skills, while others are not related to PvM or PvP. There are combat skills such as Attack, Strength, Defence and non-combat skills such as Firemaking, Woodcutting, Fishing. Runescape has been through rough patches, but it seems like the game is doing really well. At the time of writing this there are over 70 000 people online on OSRS.
